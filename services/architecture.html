Our Design Process

A structured approach to exceptional architecture

1

Discovery & Programming

We begin by understanding your needs, goals, and vision. This phase includes site analysis, program development, and establishing project parameters.

  • Client interviews and needs assessment
  • Site survey and analysis
  • Code and zoning research
  • Budget and schedule establishment
2

Schematic Design

Development of initial design concepts that address functional requirements while exploring architectural possibilities and establishing design direction.

  • Conceptual design options
  • Massing and site planning studies
  • Preliminary floor plans and elevations
  • Design review and refinement
3

Design Development

Refinement of the approved concept with detailed development of architectural elements, materials, and systems integration.

  • Detailed floor plans and sections
  • Material and finish selection
  • 3D visualization and renderings
  • Structural and MEP coordination
4

Construction Documents

Preparation of detailed technical drawings and specifications required for permit approval and construction.

  • Complete architectural drawings
  • Technical specifications
  • Permit submittal and approvals
  • Bid package preparation
5

Construction Administration

Ongoing support during construction to ensure the design is implemented correctly and quality standards are maintained.

  • Construction progress monitoring
  • Shop drawing review
  • Site visits and quality inspections
  • Project closeout and occupancy
