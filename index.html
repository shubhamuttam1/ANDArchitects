Meet Our Founder

Visionary leadership driving architectural excellence in Kanpur

Parul Uttam

Founder & Principal Architect

B.Arch (First Division) M.Arch (First Division with Distinction) Urban Planning Diploma Urban Fellows Programme

Parul Uttam brings exceptional academic credentials and specialized expertise in architectural design and urban development. With M.Arch in Recreational Architecture from Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi (2017-2019, First Division with Distinction), she combines creative design thinking with technical proficiency.

Her comprehensive educational journey includes B.Arch from Teerthanker Mahaveer University, Moradabad (2011-2016, First Division), Post Graduate Diploma in Urban Planning and Development from Indira Gandhi National Open University (2019), and Urban Fellows Programme in Urban Development from Indian Institute for Human Settlements (2021-2022). This diverse foundation in architecture, urban planning, and research methodology enables her to deliver holistic design solutions.
